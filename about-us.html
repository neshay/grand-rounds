The Team

Brittney, Iffa, Insiya and Jessica are second-year Prosthetics and Orthotics Technical students at George Brown College. We enjoy doing a deeper dive into why things are they way that they are. With this research project we decided to explore the importance of cosmesis in relation to acceptance of upper limb prosthetics by users. After conducting a literature search, we found there were some gaps/ideas we wanted to explore further. We surveyed 68 professionals within the P&O industry in Canada and have some exciting results that we have shared with you on this forum.
