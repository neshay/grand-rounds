Literature on the importance of cosmesis

It is reported that 1 in 3 upper limb amputees use a cosmetic device in some capcity (Matt et al.,2018). As stated below reasons and individual choose to use a cosmetic device os due to its appearance, comfort, and the wanting of warmth and humaness.

  • “…evidence suggests that cosmetic prostheses tend to have a higher rate of permanent use both in adult and pediatric populations.” (Dudkiewicz et al. 2004; Crandall and Tomhave 2002)

  • “Passive devices remain a popular choice for users despite their functional limitations. They are relatively inexpensive and have an aesthetically pleasing cosmetic appearance. “ (Ovadia & Askari, 2015)

  • “Passive devices are employed when physical appearance and comfort are of the utmost priority.” (Matt et al., 2018).

  • "Other desirable qualities found wanting in current prosthetic devices include lack of warmth and humanness.” (Brack & Amalu, 2021)
