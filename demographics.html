Survey Demographics

The survey was delivered electronically and distributed to professionals in the field via email. The survey included both open-ended and closed-ended questions. We sent the survey to 213 professionals across Canada and received 68 responses, yielding a 32%response rate. Through the Orthotics and Prosthetics Canada website, we obtained contact information from professionals and received responses from certified prosthetists, registered prosthetic technicians, dual certified clinicians and technicians, residents and interns.
